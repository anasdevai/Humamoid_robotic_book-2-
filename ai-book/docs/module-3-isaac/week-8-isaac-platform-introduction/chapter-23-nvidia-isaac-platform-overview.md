# Chapter 23: NVIDIA Isaac Platform Overview

**Week 8 | Module 3 | Time: ~3 hours**

## Learning Objectives
- Understand the components and architecture of the NVIDIA Isaac Platform.
- Identify the role of Isaac Sim, Isaac ROS, and Jetson in robotics development.
- Recognize the benefits of using NVIDIA Isaac for AI-robotics applications.

## Prerequisites
- Completion of Module 2: Digital Twin.
- Basic understanding of GPU computing and AI/ML.

## Overview
This chapter provides a comprehensive overview of the NVIDIA Isaac Platform, a powerful suite of tools for accelerating AI-robotics development. We will explore its key components, including Isaac Sim for photorealistic simulation, Isaac ROS for hardware-accelerated ROS 2 packages, and the Jetson platform for edge AI deployment. The chapter will highlight the benefits of using NVIDIA Isaac for developing and deploying intelligent robots.

## Key Concepts
- NVIDIA Isaac ecosystem
- Isaac Sim, Isaac ROS, Jetson
- Hardware-accelerated robotics
- AI at the edge

## What You'll Build
No hands-on component in this introductory chapter.
