# Chapter 25: Photorealistic Simulation

**Week 8 | Module 3 | Time: ~3.5 hours**

## Learning Objectives
- Leverage Isaac Sim's rendering capabilities for photorealistic environments.
- Understand the importance of high-fidelity visuals for perception training.
- Configure lighting, materials, and textures for realistic scene rendering.

## Prerequisites
- Chapter 24: Isaac Sim Setup and Configuration.

## Overview
This chapter explores the advanced rendering capabilities of NVIDIA Isaac Sim, focusing on creating photorealistic simulation environments. We will delve into the importance of high-fidelity visuals for training robust perception systems in AI robotics. Practical techniques for configuring lighting, materials, and textures will be covered, enabling you to build visually convincing digital twins that closely resemble real-world scenarios.

## Key Concepts
- Isaac Sim rendering pipeline
- Photorealism in simulation
- Lighting models and effects
- Material and texture configuration
- Synthetic data generation for computer vision

## What You'll Build
- A photorealistic Isaac Sim scene with a robot.
- Experiment with different lighting and material settings.
