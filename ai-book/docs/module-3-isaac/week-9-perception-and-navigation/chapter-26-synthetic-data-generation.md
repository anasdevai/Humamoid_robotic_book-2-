# Chapter 26: Synthetic Data Generation

**Week 9 | Module 3 | Time: ~4 hours**

## Learning Objectives
- Understand the benefits and challenges of synthetic data for AI training.
- Utilize Isaac Sim to generate high-quality synthetic datasets for computer vision tasks.
- Apply domain randomization techniques to improve model generalization.

## Prerequisites
- Chapter 25: Photorealistic Simulation.

## Overview
This chapter delves into the critical area of synthetic data generation, a powerful technique for training AI models in robotics. We will explore the benefits and challenges of using synthetically generated data, focusing on how to leverage Isaac Sim to create high-quality datasets for various computer vision tasks. Practical application of domain randomization techniques will be covered to ensure that models trained on synthetic data generalize effectively to real-world scenarios.

## Key Concepts
- Synthetic data advantages and limitations
- Isaac Sim for data generation
- Domain randomization
- Data augmentation
- Annotation generation

## What You'll Build
- A pipeline for generating synthetic datasets in Isaac Sim.
- A small dataset with randomized properties.
