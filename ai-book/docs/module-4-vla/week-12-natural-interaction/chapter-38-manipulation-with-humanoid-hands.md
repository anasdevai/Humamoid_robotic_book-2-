# Chapter 38: Manipulation with Humanoid Hands

**Week 12 | Module 4 | Time: ~4 hours**

## Learning Objectives
- Understand the complexities of dexterous manipulation with multi-fingered humanoid hands.
- Explore advanced grasping techniques for diverse objects.
- Implement a control strategy for fine manipulation tasks.

## Prerequisites
- Chapter 37: Natural Human-Robot Interaction Design.
- Chapter 36: Manipulation and Grasping.

## Overview
Building on fundamental grasping concepts, this chapter explores advanced manipulation techniques specifically for multi-fingered humanoid hands. We will delve into the complexities of dexterous manipulation, examining strategies for grasping and interacting with diverse objects. The focus is on implementing sophisticated control strategies for fine manipulation tasks, enabling humanoids to perform intricate operations with precision and adaptability.

## Key Concepts
- Dexterous manipulation
- Multi-fingered grasping
- Force control and impedance control
- Object recognition for manipulation
- In-hand manipulation

## What You'll Build
- A simulated humanoid hand performing a complex manipulation task.
- Implement a control loop for fine object positioning.
