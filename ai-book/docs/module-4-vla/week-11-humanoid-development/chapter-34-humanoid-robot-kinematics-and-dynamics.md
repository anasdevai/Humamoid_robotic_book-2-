# Chapter 34: Humanoid Robot Kinematics and Dynamics

**Week 11 | Module 4 | Time: ~4 hours**

## Learning Objectives
- Understand forward and inverse kinematics for humanoid robots.
- Analyze humanoid robot dynamics, including equations of motion.
- Implement basic kinematic control for a multi-joint humanoid arm.

## Prerequisites
- Chapter 33: Sim-to-Real Transfer Techniques.
- Strong understanding of linear algebra and rigid body mechanics.

## Overview
This chapter introduces the fundamental concepts of kinematics and dynamics as applied to humanoid robots. We will explore forward and inverse kinematics, essential for understanding robot pose and joint configurations. The chapter will also delve into humanoid robot dynamics, including equations of motion and force analysis, culminating in the practical implementation of basic kinematic control for a multi-joint humanoid arm.

## Key Concepts
- Forward kinematics
- Inverse kinematics
- Jacobian matrix
- Rigid body dynamics
- Equations of motion

## What You'll Build
- A Python script for humanoid arm forward kinematics.
- Implement a simple inverse kinematics solver.
