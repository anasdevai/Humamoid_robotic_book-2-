# Chapter 46: Understanding Hardware Requirements

**Hardware & Lab Setup | Time: ~2 hours**

## Learning Objectives
- Identify the essential hardware components for Physical AI development.
- Understand the specifications and trade-offs for different hardware choices (CPU, GPU, RAM, storage).
- Plan a hardware setup based on project needs and budget.

## Prerequisites
- Basic understanding of computer hardware.

## Overview
This chapter provides a foundational understanding of the hardware requirements for Physical AI development. We will identify essential components, discuss the specifications and trade-offs of various CPU, GPU, RAM, and storage options, and guide you through planning a hardware setup that aligns with your specific project needs and budget, from cloud-based solutions to dedicated workstations.

## Key Concepts
- CPU vs. GPU for AI
- RAM and storage considerations
- Edge AI hardware
- Workstation specifications
- Cloud computing for robotics

## What You'll Build
- A detailed hardware requirement list for your specific Physical AI project.
