# Chapter 50: Cloud vs On-Premise Lab Setup

**Hardware & Lab Setup | Time: ~2 hours**

## Learning Objectives
- Compare and contrast cloud-native and on-premise lab setups for Physical AI.
- Understand the cost implications and scalability of each approach.
- Choose an optimal lab setup based on resources and project requirements.

## Prerequisites
- Chapter 49: Robot Hardware Options.

## Overview
This chapter provides a comparative analysis of cloud-native versus on-premise lab setups for Physical AI development. We will explore the advantages, disadvantages, cost implications, and scalability of each approach, helping you make informed decisions. The goal is to guide you in choosing an optimal lab setup that best fits your available resources, project requirements, and long-term development strategy.

## Key Concepts
- Cloud robotics platforms (e.g., AWS RoboMaker, Google Cloud Robotics)
- On-premise lab advantages
- Cost analysis and budgeting for lab setups
- Scalability and flexibility
- Hybrid approaches

## What You'll Build
- A decision matrix for your lab setup choice.
- A preliminary budget for your chosen setup.
